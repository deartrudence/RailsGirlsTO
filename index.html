About Us

Calling all female Rails developers! Whether you are a veteran developer or just starting out, this group is for you. Rails Girls TO is a volunteer-led community group, focussed on helping females learn Ruby on Rails and web development. Our initiatives aim to foster growth of the female developer community in Toronto, and provide opportunities for women to improve their coding skills and network with others.

We are dedicated to providing harassment-free learning experiences for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion. Men are also welcomed to attend our events, and are highly encouraged to bring a female friend with them.

Join us on Meetup and Slack, and follow us on Twitter @railsgirlsTO!

HackerYou - Lightening talks about stepping up your Rails game

Now that you've completed a Bootcamp or Part-time course for Ruby on Rails, how do you continue to grow as a Rails developer?

During a coding course it's easy to be motivated by everyone around you and everything you're learning is dictated by the course content. There are mentors and teachers around everyday to answer any questions that might come up. But how do you keep scaling up once all of the course comes to an end?

This meetup we'll hear from several developers who have completed a coding bootcamp talk about expanding their Rails knowledge, whether it be deep diving into a specific topic, or how to best keep motivated to get projects completed. These talks will be a combination of technical knowledge and general philosphies helping beginners to jump to the next level.
